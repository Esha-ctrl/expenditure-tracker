Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account with Spendly, we collect basic information such as your name, email address, and a securely hashed password. As you use the Service, we also store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions — so that we can display them back to you. We do not collect payment information, bank credentials, or data from any external financial accounts.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Service: to authenticate you when you sign in, to display your expense history, and to generate summaries and category breakdowns. We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for marketing purposes. Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics may be used to improve product features.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secure database with access restricted to authorized systems. Passwords are never stored in plain text; they are hashed using industry-standard algorithms. We retain your data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, your associated expense records are removed from our active systems within a reasonable period.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on a small number of third-party services for hosting, fonts, and basic infrastructure. These providers process data only on our behalf and under contractual obligations to keep it confidential. We do not embed third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising scripts that would share your personal data with outside parties.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, the data we hold about you, or you would like to request export or deletion of your account data, please get in touch at privacy@spendly.example. We aim to respond to all privacy-related requests within a reasonable timeframe.
